Math Courses

Here is a non-exhaustive list about the math courses I have taken (and enjoyed).

Current

  • Functional Analysis, Autumn 2019

    • Topics include: Normed vector spaces, Banach spaces, Soblev spaces, Hilbert spaces, Hahn-Banach theorem, Banach open mapping theorem, uniform boundedness principle, and convex sets

  • Theory and Methods for Statistical Inference, Autumn 2019 (graduate course)

    • Topics include: hypothesis testing and estimation, inference and information geometry, large deviation and asymptotic analysis, and inference algorithms

  • Numerical Analysis, Autumn 2019

    • Topics include: direct and iterative methods for solving linear systems, iterative methods for nonlinear systems, eigenvalue problems, interpolation, numerical integration and differentiation, and ordinary differential equations

  • Differential Geometry, Autumn 2019

    • Topics include: local theory of curves and surfaces, fundamental theorems and intrinsic geometry of surfaces, and selected topics on global geometry

Past

  • Convex Optimization, Spring 2019 (graduate course)

  • Textbook: “Convex Optimization” by S. Boyd

  • Measure and Integral, Spring 2019

  • Probability Theory, Spring 2018

Readings

  • “Fundamentals of wireless communication” by D. Tse and P. Viswanath
