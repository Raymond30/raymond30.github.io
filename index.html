About me

I am a 1st-year Ph.D. student in the Department of ECE at UT Austin, advised by Prof. Aryan Mokhtari. Before UT, I received a B.E. degree in Electronic Engineering and a B.S. degree in Mathematics both from Tsinghua University in 2020.

Broadly speaking, my research interests lie in the theory of optimization and machine learning. Most recently, I am developing novel algorithms for minimax optimization and studying their applications in game-theoretic learning.
