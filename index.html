About me

I am a 2nd-year Ph.D. student in the Department of ECE at UT Austin, advised by Prof. Aryan Mokhtari. Before UT, I received a B.E. degree in Electronic Engineering and a B.S. degree in Mathematics both from Tsinghua University in 2020.

My current research interests lie in convex and non-convex optimization as well as the applications in signal processing and machine learning problems. Most recently, I am developing novel algorithms for minimax optimization and online learning.
